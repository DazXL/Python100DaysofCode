The Best Movies According to DAZ

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Lord of The Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

One of the best movies of all time. It has a great story, amazing characters, and stunning visuals. It is a must-watch for any movie lover.

The Lord of The Rings: The Two Towers

The second installment in the Lord of the Rings trilogy. It continues the epic story and introduces new characters and plot twists. It is just as good as the first movie, if not better.

The Lord of The Rings: The Return of the King

The final installment in the Lord of the Rings trilogy. It is an epic conclusion to the story and features some of the most memorable scenes in movie history. It is a must-watch for any fan of the series.
